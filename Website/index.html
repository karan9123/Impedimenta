About

Impedimenta is a decentralized cloud designed to give you a more secure way to store your data by encryption and sharding.

We start by encrypting your data before it is sent to the blockchain.

Once the data is encrypted, we shard it. Sharding is the process of separating the data into smaller pieces. You can think of sharding like pieces of a puzzle that can only be complete with every piece attached, but all the pieces are hidden in a throughout your house. This adds an extra layer of security.
